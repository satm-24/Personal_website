I’m currently trying to master the basics of frontend technologies and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, while building my skills in Java, which I’d say is my strongest language. Some of my other interests include sports, video games, weightlifting, and music. Though I wasn’t born in the states and moved around a lot as a kid, I’m proud to call myself a true Bostonian and I’m a HUGE Patriots, Red Sox, Celtics, and Bruins fan. I’m grateful to have the opportunity to go to school in my home city, and being at Northeastern has given me a lot more opportunities to network and explore.
